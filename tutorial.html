Lifetopia World
Farming Vertical Slice · Mini Tutorial

1. Prerequisites

Before playing, make sure you are set up:
  • Wallet network set to Solana Devnet
  • Some Devnet SOL tokens in your wallet
  • Get free Devnet SOL from faucet.solana.com

2. Getting Started

  1. Click the Connect button on the main screen and connect your Solana wallet.
  2. Select the Farm Map from the menu to enter the world.

3. Controls

Move and explore the world:
  • WASD or ←↑↓→ move your character.
  • M opens and closes the World Map.
  • Backspace opens and closes your Backpack.
  • Use 1–9 or mouse scroll to change the active slot.

4. Farming Loop

  1. Use the Shovel on the ground to till the land.
  2. Use the Watering Can on the tilled land.
  3. Stand near a river or pool to recharge watering energy.
  4. Plant seeds on watered soil.
  5. Harvest fully grown crops with the Scythe.

5. Collecting Resources

Use the correct tool for each object:
  • Grass with the Scythe.
  • Rocks with the Pickaxe.
  • Trees with the Axe.
  • Pick up loot to add collectibles to your inventory.

6. Shops & Economy

  • Click NPCs to talk or open their shop.
  • Use gold to buy new seeds and tools.
  • Press Sell to enter Sell Mode, then left-click items in your inventory to sell them.

7. Extras

  • Autosave keeps your progress updated while you play.
  • Claim your Starter Pack NFT to receive starter seeds.
